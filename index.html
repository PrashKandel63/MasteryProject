Achieving My Career Goals in Video Game Design

Becoming a Professional Video Game Developer

As I approach the final stages of my Mastery Journey at Full Sail University, my career goal is to become a professional video game designer. My passion is creating immersive and innovative experiences for players around the world.

Graduation Goal: Launching My First Indie Game

Within six months of graduation, I aim to launch my first indie game. This game will serve as both a portfolio piece and a foundation for future projects. It will showcase my design skills and highlight my ability to execute a full project.
